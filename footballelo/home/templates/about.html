About

This site is based of the ELO system developed by Dr.Arpad. This is the system used in chess. A modified version is used here for the top football clubs of Europe.

Here's rating based on the following formula's
Rn=R0+KÃ—(W-We)

Where Rn is the new rating, R0 is the old ratings. K is the weighting constant, here league matches are weighted at 30. W is the result, with wins being worth 1.0, draws are worth 0.5, and losses are worth 0.0 . WE is the expected results. Its calculated with 1/(10-dr/400+1). dr is the difference of ELO ratings.
